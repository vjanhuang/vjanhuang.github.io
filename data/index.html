Utrecht Multi-Person Motion Benchmark

The UMPM Benchmark is a collection of video recordings together with a ground truth based on motion capture data. It is intended to be used for assessing the quality of methods for recognition of poses from multiple persons from video data, both using a single or multiple cameras. More details in there. Moreover, you can download it from my Google Drive.
